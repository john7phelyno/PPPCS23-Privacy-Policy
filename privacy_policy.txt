Pembimbing Pengkhotbah PCS ("the Owner") is the Data Controller and is responsible for determining the purposes and means of processing Personal Data in connection with this Application.

---

## Types of Data Collected

This Application does **not** collect, store, or process any Personal Data directly.

The Application does not require Users to create accounts, submit personal information, or provide identifiable data. No forms, user profiles, or in-app data submissions are implemented.

Any data associated with the use of this Application is limited strictly to information automatically collected by **Google Play Console** and related Google services, in accordance with Google’s own policies.

Such data may include aggregated and anonymized information such as:

* App installation and uninstallation statistics
* Device type and operating system version
* Crash reports and performance diagnostics
* General usage metrics

This data is collected and processed solely by Google and is not accessible to, stored by, or controlled directly by the Owner.

Since the Application itself does not collect Personal Data, no Data is required from Users for the normal operation of the Application.

## Retention Time

Personal Data shall be processed and stored for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected.

Accordingly:

* Personal Data collected for purposes related to the performance of a contract between the Owner and the User shall be retained until the contract has been fully performed.
* Personal Data collected for the purposes of the Owner’s legitimate interests shall be retained for as long as needed to fulfill those purposes.
* Where consent has been given, the Owner may retain Personal Data until such consent is withdrawn.
* The Owner may be required to retain Personal Data for a longer period in order to comply with legal obligations or orders from public authorities.

Once the applicable retention period expires, Personal Data shall be deleted or anonymized. After this point, the rights of access, erasure, rectification, and data portability may no longer be enforceable.

## Rights of Users

Users may exercise certain rights regarding their Personal Data processed by the Owner. These rights include:

* The right to withdraw consent at any time.
* The right to object to the processing of Personal Data where the processing is based on a legal ground other than consent.
* The right to access Personal Data and obtain information about its processing.
* The right to verify accuracy and request rectification of inaccurate or incomplete Data.
* The right to restrict processing under certain circumstances.
* The right to obtain the erasure of Personal Data, where applicable.
* The right to receive Personal Data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format and, where technically feasible, to have it transmitted to another controller.
* The right to lodge a complaint with a competent data protection authority.

### Right to Object to Processing

Where Personal Data is processed for reasons of public interest, official authority, or legitimate interests, Users may object to such processing by providing grounds related to their particular situation.

Where Personal Data is processed for direct marketing purposes, Users may object at any time without providing any justification.
